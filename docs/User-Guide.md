# How to Get the Re:START Profile Widget!

Welcome! This guide will show you exactly how to add the Re:START profile widget to your Discord account. It only takes a few seconds, and once you set it up, you can customize your profile stats anytime!

---

### Step 1: Set Your First Stat
To get started, you just need to tell the bot what you want to display on your profile.

In any channel where the bot is, type the `/setstat` command. 
For example, if you want your 1st slot to show your Vibe:
> `/setstat slot:1 title:Vibe value:Chill`

![Screenshot showing the user typing the /setstat command](PUT_YOUR_SCREENSHOT_HERE.png)

---

### Step 2: Authorize the Bot
Because the widget displays on *your personal profile*, Discord requires you to give the bot permission to update it. 

After running the command, the bot will reply with a temporary authorization link. 
1. Click the **"Click here to Authorize"** link.
2. Discord will pop up a window asking if you want to connect your account to Re:START. 
3. Click **Authorize**.

![Screenshot showing the bot's embed with the Authorize link](PUT_YOUR_SCREENSHOT_HERE.png)
![Screenshot of the Discord Authorization pop-up screen](PUT_YOUR_SCREENSHOT_HERE.png)

> **Note:** You only have to do this once! After authorizing, the bot will forever be able to update your widget whenever you use the command.

---

### Step 3: Check Your Profile!
That's it! Your stat was pushed, and the widget is now permanently unlocked on your account.

To see it:
1. Click your name in the chat to open your profile.
2. Scroll down, and you will see the **Re:START** widget!

![Screenshot of the beautiful widget sitting on a user's Discord profile](PUT_YOUR_SCREENSHOT_HERE.png)

### Troubleshooting
**"Why does my widget say game stats are still syncing?"**
If you see a syncing message, it just means you haven't filled out all your slots yet! Run `/setstat` for slots 2 through 6 to fully customize your card.

**"I don't see the widget on my profile!"**
Make sure your privacy settings allow Discord to show apps on your profile. 
Go to **User Settings (gear icon) -> Activity Privacy** and make sure **"Share your detected activities with others"** is toggled **ON**.
